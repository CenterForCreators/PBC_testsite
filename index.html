Technology

The Performance
Intelligence Layer

Six integrated systems work together in real time. No single tool. No bolt on feature. Every piece connects to your personal data, your goals, and your game. AR performance glasses bring the data directly into your field of vision while you compete.

Holographic Court Instruction
Life size holographic instruction overlays court positioning, shot placement, and movement patterns in real time. You see exactly where to be and why before you make the next move.
Live Pro Player Avatars
World class professional pickleball players broadcast live lessons as streaming avatars simultaneously to every PBC location. Real coaching. Real time. Available to every member regardless of location.
Real Time 3D Shot Analysis
Every swing, every placement, every trajectory is captured and analyzed in three dimensions as it happens. Instant feedback means faster improvement with nothing falling through the cracks.
Personal AI Performance Agent
Every member gets their own AI agent trained on their performance history, physical data, and personal goals. It builds a custom plan, tracks progress, and adapts as you improve. This is not a generic chatbot. It knows your game.
Mindset and Mental Performance
Purpose built mental performance programming is woven into the member experience from the start. Visualization, competitive mindset training, and focus protocols developed alongside the physical game plan.
Biometric Movement Intelligence
Wearable and in facility sensors capture movement patterns, reaction time, physical output, and recovery data. Your body tells the system what your eyes cannot see. The AI uses that data to optimize every session.
AR Performance Glasses
Heads-up display lenses overlay real-time shot trajectory, court positioning, and AI coaching cues directly into your field of vision while you compete. Every rally becomes a training session without ever leaving the court.
